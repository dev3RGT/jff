Our Green Story

A sanctuary where plants and people thrive

"When we first imagined JFF Plant Shop, it was never just about opening a store. It was about creating a small green sanctuary."

We are a boutique business with a strong focus on houseplants, indoor greenery, and creating a relaxed environment. Whether you're looking for the perfect monstera, expert horticultural advice, or just a great cup of coffee surrounded by nature — you'll find it here.

Every plant in our collection is handpicked and cared for with intention. We believe that living with plants changes how you feel at home, and we're here to help you find exactly the right one.
